## Navy showers

Navy showers are simple.

1. Turn on the water.
2. Get wet.
3. Turn off the water.
4. Lather up.
5. Turn on the water.
6. Rinse off.
7. Do not repeat.

It's effective.
It uses
very little water.
But it takes away
a luxury
that must
of us
are used to
and deeply miss.
You can't linger.
You can't
take your time
and enjoy
the warm water
flowing over you.

## Recirculating shower

Long lingering showers
are simply
not possible
in a campervan
without hookups
unless you recycle
the water.

It turns out
that recycling
the water
in your shower
is viable.
Several DIYers
have built
working recirculating showers.
One couple [TK link]
tested their water
after reusing
the same
2 gallons [TK confirm volume]
for a week [TK confirm time]
and it
came back safe.

Multiple retailers
in the US [TK link to retailer]
are working

for campervans.

A recirculating shower adds
quite a bit
of complexity.
The filters need
to be cleaned
every week
or so.
And petroleum-based
soaps and shampoos
must be avoided.
But this seems
well worth it
to be able
to take
hour long showers
in your van.
