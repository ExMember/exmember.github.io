Ry,
my 2022 Thor Rize,
(now [for sale](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale))
has a couch
that converts
to a bed.
It is
72 by 41 inches
when converted,
plenty spacious
for most people
and sufficient
for a loving couple.

It works,
but I need
a permanent bed.

## Seams

When the couch
is converted
to a bed,
the sleeping surface
is primarily
made up
of four
different cushions.

The seam between
those cushions
are directly down
and across
the middle
of the bed
and can be
uncomfortable,
especially if
you turn
in a way
that puts
a lot
of your weight
on a seam.

## Conversion

In addition,
before going
to sleep,
you need to
convert the couch
to a bed.
In theory,
you could
leave it
as a bed
all the time.
But because
it blocks
the hallway
through the van,
you will probably
want to wait
until bed time.

It not hard
or time-consuming
but it is
one more thing
to do
before bed.

## Sleep pad

A sleeping pad
helps.

I bought
the [DeepSleep Duo](https://www.expedusa.com/products/deepsleep-mat-duo-2025)
from Exped.
It adds
more cushioning,
covers up
the seams,
and overall
makes the bed
far more comfortable.

Unfortunately,
it is
one more thing
to deal with
before going
to bed
and after
getting up.
It takes time
to get
into position,
unroll
and fill up
with air.
It takes
even more time
to deflate,
roll up,
and squeeze into
its storage bag.

It also
takes up
a considerable amount
of storage space.
Deflated
and rolled up,
it still
wouldn't fit
in any drawers
or cabinets.
I stored it
in the closet
in the bathroom.

## Half-bed

The other thing
I tried
was sleeping
on the couch
without expanding it
to a bed.

You don't
have to use
all four cushions.
Sleeping
on just two
makes it
much faster
to convert
from a couch
to a bed.
I even found
that I could
leave the sheets
in place
covered
with a blanket.

The result
is a bed
that is very small
but big enough.
The trade off
was well worth it
for me
when solo camping
after consecutive
long days
of snowboarding.

## Permanent bed

I need
a more comfortable bed
that I don't
have to convert
when I
go to bed
and unconvert
when I get up.
My campervan needs
a permanent bed.

That is
one reason
I am
[selling my campervan](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale).
It doesn't meet
all of
[my needs](/my-van-needs).
For example,
I need
[a separating toilet](/my-van-needs-a-separating-toilet).
