In January 2025
I bought
a campervan,
a 2023 (check TK) Thor Rize
that I named "Ry."

After 18 months
of ownership
and 50 nights together
on the road,
I have concluded
that van life,
at least in this van,
doesn't work
for me.

Now that
it's time
to sell Ry,
I thought
I should
first catalogue
what went wrong.
In truth,
the dream
of van life
is still
very real
for me
and this experiment
with it
was full
of positive experiences.
But there are
some things
that need
to be different
before I can live
my van life dream.

1. My Van Life Dream
2. More Maintainable
2. Separating Toilet
2. Stealthier
3. No Propane
4. Long, hot, showers
5. Four Wheel Drive
6. External Storage
7. Better Bed (permanent, comfier)
8. Someone to Travel With
