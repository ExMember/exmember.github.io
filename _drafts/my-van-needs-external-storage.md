Ry,
my [TK year] Thor Rize,
has plenty
of storage.

## Factory-installed storage

There are
three large cabinets,
on the driver side,
shelving and drawers
behind them.
On the passenger side
there is
another cabinet, more drawers,
and a microwave
that is great
for storing
plates
and paper towels.
In the bathroom,
there is
a medicine cabinet,
large drawers
and a closet,
along with
a large shelf,
and some netting
accessible
from the back.
And that's
in addition to
the "pizza oven" shelf
above the cab
and two
large glove comparments
on the passenger side.

But it's still
difficult
to find space
inside the van
for some things.

## Stinky slinky

Thor installs
a grey water hose
compartment
underneath
the seating area
that is accessible
from outside
and keeps
grey water
from dripping
on anything inside.

I removed
the compartment
to make space
for my lithium battery
upgrade. [TK battery upgrade link]

Even if I hadn't,
I also upgraded
to a better quality
grey water hose
that wouldn't fit
in that compartment.

## Snowboard

Ry has
numerous storage areas,
but none
are big enough
for a snowboard.

My snowboard fit
pretty well
on the floor
behind the seats.
But that location
created a trip hazard
when entering
or exiting
the cab.

It also required
making sure
there was
no snow or slush
on the board
before putting
it away,
otherwise
the melting snow
would drip
onto the floor.

My campervan need
external storage.
