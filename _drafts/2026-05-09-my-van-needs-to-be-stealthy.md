People who sleep
in their vehicles
are unwelcome
in a lot of places.

The more urban
the area is
and the closer
it is
to dense
vacation spots
the more people
will be
on the lookout
for people
sleeping in vehicles
and making
and effort
to git rid
of them.

A lot of the uses
of my van
in [my van life dream]()
involve camping
in urban areas
and near
popular vacation spots.

There is
a lot
you can do
and a lot
I did do
to avoid
getting hassled.
But it all
boils down
techniques to avoid
people noticing that
you are sleeping
in a vehicle.

It would help
if my vehicle
looked less like
a vehicle
that people
sleep in.

Blackout shades help,
thought I wish
they were integrated
with the windows.

A bulkhead divider
behind the cab
might help too.

Replacing the roof fan
and air conditioner
with lower-profile alternatives
helped a little.

But from outside
the solar panel, AC, and vents
are clearly visible
on the roof.
Multiple connectors, doors, and exhausts
are visible
on the side.
Along with
the Rize badge
and RV insignia.

My van needs to be stealthier.
