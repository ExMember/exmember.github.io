Graeber and Wengrow perpetually\
Studied humanity's history\

&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And sought to find\
The origins of inequality.

Because of how they relate\
Many believe and state:\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The answer's the same\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If they could name\
The origins of the state.

The history commonly viewed\
That from this it all ensued:\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Farming revolution\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So groups were producin'\

Allowed for specialization:\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Soldiers, police, baronets;\

All forms of domination.

A path that's irreversible.\
