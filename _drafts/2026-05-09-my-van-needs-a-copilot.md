I bought Ry,
my 2022 Thor Rize,
(now [for sale](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale))
with plans
to do
a lot
of vacationing, traveling, and camping
on my own.

And I did.
It was great.
As a single person
and an introvert
I had
a phenomenal time.

But life
on the road
is especially isolating.
And more than that,
I've changed.
I want
to do
my vacationing, traveling, and camping
with people
who are close to me.

Let's be clear,
I don't expect
to find
a partner
to join
[my van life dream](/my-vanlife-dream)
as is.
I'm very clear
on [my needs
for van life.](/my-van-needs)
Another person's needs
could be
entirely different.
And with two
of us
in one van,
our needs
could be
completely different
again.

I did this alone.
I'm glad I did
but for it
to be something
I want
to continue,
my van needs
a copilot.

This is
one of
the reasons
I am
[selling my campervan](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale).
It's not right
for me
anymore.
Maybe
it's right
for you.
Read up
on [the van](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale)
and make me
an offer.
