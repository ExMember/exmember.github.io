Ry,
my [TK year] Thor Rize,
started out
as a Ram Promaster
but was converted
to a campervan
by Thor Motor Coach
in their Indiana factories.

A company
like Thor
can do
a lot
of things
that smaller
or custom builders
can't do
at a reasonable price.
For example,
the floor
of the wet bath
in the Thor Rize
is perfectly shaped
to fit around
the Thretford [model TK]
cassette toilet.

The downside
of that type
of fit and finish
is that
if you want to
remove the cassette toilet
and replace it
with a separating toilet [link TK]
you need
to replace
the entire
bathroom floor.

Custom fittings
like that
also make repairs
more difficult.
Twice,
I had a leak
in the van
caused by
a hose connection
shaking loose.
In both cases
the fix was
extremely simple,
grab the connector
and screw
it on
a little bit tighter.

However,
in both cases,
the loose hose
was behind
a custom fit wall
and required removing
two dozen screws,
and lowering
the ceiling
to get to it.

My van needs
to be maintainable.
