Day Four: Fall Asleep and Be Blinded Away

10 hours. +$124

Had some problems staying focused, especially through a few incredibly long stretches of bad cards. As a result, I probably didn’t make the best decisions I could have when I was in the hand.

Right away I can remember two horribly played hands, 8To and AJo.

I was up as much as $300 before having most of my profits blinded away. In a effort to get it back I also played a lot longer than I should have. I’m going to be paying for that today.
