I am starring in The Resurrection by W. B. Yeats opening this weekend. This is a very short, but extremely challenging, lesser-known one-act by one of the great writers of the last century. It has been very exciting to work on and I guarantee the show will be unique and worthwhile.

If you'd like to come see it, we are running from March 28th through April 6th, Fridays and Saturdays at 7:30pm, and Sundays at 4:00pm at the First Presbyterian Church of Santa Monica, 1220 Second Street, Santa Monica CA, 90401</a>. Tickets are $10 "or donation" and there is plenty of public parking nearby, as well as pubs where we can drink off the weight of the show afterwards.
