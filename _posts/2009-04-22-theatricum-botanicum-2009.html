This summer I will be in Cymbeline and A Midsummer Night's Dream at Will Geer's Theatricum Botanicum in Topanga.

The Theatricum Botanicum is an outdoor theater with a huge stage integrated with the surrounding woods and trails. It is the only stage I have ever seen that is suitable for performing Shakespeare, so it's quite a treat to be a part of the company.

We perform for our first school group on Monday April 27 but don't open for the public until Sunday May 31.

All the details on at Theatricum.com</a>. Come and see us!
