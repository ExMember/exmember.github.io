"The Olivia Experiment" opens Friday August 22nd at the Laemmle Music Hall for an exclusive one-week engagement.

After the 7:20 showing opening night there will be a Q&A with lead actress Skye Noel, screenwriter Alexandra Kominsaruk, director Sonja Schenk, and cinematographer William MacCollum.

Saturday the 23rd will feature free CDs with music from the band Small Sails who are featured on "The Olivia Experiment" soundtrack.
