I'm selling my campervan, a 2022 Thor Rize 18M with 12k miles.

Asking \$75,000.

<figure>
  <a href="/images/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale/hero.jpg">
    <img src="/images/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale/hero.thumbnail.jpg" alt="Campervan, front passenger, three-quarter view">
    <figcaption>2022 Thor Rize 18M, hero shot</figcaption>
  </a>
</figure>

Highlights:
- 12,000 miles
- Well-maintained, service records available
- No smoking, no vaping, no pets
- Good gas mileage (approx. 16 mpg combined / 20 mpg highway)
- California registration valid to May 2027

Lowlights:
- Nicks and scratches in the exterior
- Floor scratches
- No awning

Thor factory install:
- Large windows
- Dual swivel seats
- Convertible couch / bed
- Moveable / removable table
- Refrigerator (12V DC)
- Gas stove (propane)
- Microwave
- Kitchen sink
- Shower
- Cassette toilet
- Combination heater and water heater (propane)
- Electric generator (propane)
- Rear bike rack

Upgrades:
- Upgraded 300Ah lithium battery
- Upgraded 12V AC (with heat pump)
- Upgraded MaxxAir roof fan
- WeBoost cell-signal booster with rooftop antenna
- Custom Method wheels
- Blackout window shades from Wanderful and Vanmade Gear

Over the past 16 months I spent 50 nights in this van, on the beach, in the
mountains, in campgrounds, and stealth camping in urban and suburban areas.
I had a blast, but I learned that I like to travel farther than I like to drive.
And while I love the flexibility of being able to leave at the drop of a hat and
take my home with me, I need to plan everything out anyway.

The chassis, a high roof 2021 Ram Promaster, drives like an SUV. The short
wheelbase (118in) and tight turning radius makes it easy to maneuver in the
city. And the small footprint (smaller than a Cadillac Escalade), means it can
park in any standard-sized space.

Inside there is room to stand up and walk around and a separate full wet bath
with a shower, toilet, and second sink. Dual captain's chairs provide extra
seating and the bench converts to a bed that sleeps two.

VIN: 3C6LRVBG1ME573441

Payment via ACH, wire transfer, credit card, or Zelle all through [KeySavvy](https://www.keysavvy.com/pay/ram-fhaecj).
