Cops shoot and then sing the same song,\
"We'll find out what really went on."\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;They'll investigate\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And after a wait\
Declare the cops did nothing wrong.
