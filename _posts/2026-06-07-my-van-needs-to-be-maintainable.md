Ry,
my 2022 Thor Rize campervan,
(now [for sale](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale))
started out
as a Ram Promaster
but was converted
to a campervan
by Thor Motor Coach
in their Indiana factory.

A company
like Thor
can do
a lot
of things
that smaller builders
and custom builders
cannot do
at a reasonable price.
For example,
the floor
of the wet bath
in the Thor Rize
is perfectly shaped
to fit around
the Thretford
cassette toilet.

The downside
of that type
of fit and finish
is that
if you want to
remove the cassette toilet
and replace it
with a [separating toilet](/my-van-needs-a-separating-toilet)
you need
to replace
the entire
bathroom floor.

Custom fittings
like that
also make repairs
more difficult.
Twice,
I had a leak
in the van
caused by
a hose connection
shaking loose.
In both cases
the fix was
extremely simple,
grab the connector
and screw
it on
a little bit tighter.

However,
in both cases,
the loose hose
was behind
a custom fit wall
and required removing
two dozen screws,
and lowering
the ceiling
to get to it.

My van needs
to be maintainable.

This is
one of
the reasons
I am
[selling my campervan](/thor-rize-campervan-for-sale).
It doesn't meet
all of
[my needs](/my-van-needs).
For example,
my van needs
[to be stealthy](/my-van-needs-to-be-stealthy).
