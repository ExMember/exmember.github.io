On the fourth Thursday
of November,
we celebrate
a harvest feast
in honor
of the Wampanoag
and all the nations
of Turtle Island,
whose knowledge, wealth, and generosity
made it possible
for immigrants
to survive
and thrive here.

Happy Thanksgiving.
